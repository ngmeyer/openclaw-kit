# OpenClawKit: Branding Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Name: OpenClawKit
- **Rationale:** Combines "OpenClaw" with "Kit" to communicate a complete installation package
- **Usage:** Always as one word with capital C and K: "OpenClawKit" (not "Open Claw Kit")
- **Tagline:** "OpenClaw AI, Simplified"

### Core Values
- **Simplicity:** Making complex technology accessible to everyone
- **Empowerment:** Giving non-technical users access to powerful AI tools
- **Reliability:** Dependable installation that just works

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **Deep Blue:** #1E3A8A 
  - Conveys trust, technology, and reliability
  - Used for headers, buttons, and key UI elements
- **Vibrant Coral:** #FB7C4A
  - Adds warmth and approachability
  - Used for accents, highlights, and call-to-action elements

#### Secondary Colors
- **Light Blue:** #BFD1FF
  - Creates breathing room and lightness
  - Used for backgrounds, cards, and secondary elements
- **Deep Navy:** #172554
  - Adds depth and sophistication
  - Used for text and footers

#### Neutral Colors
- **White:** #FFFFFF
- **Light Gray:** #F2F2F2
- **Medium Gray:** #CCCCCC
- **Dark Gray:** #4A4A4A

### Typography

#### Primary Font: Inter
- Clean, modern sans-serif font
- Excellent readability on screens
- Weights: Regular (400), Medium (500), and Bold (700)

#### Secondary Font: SF Pro (system font)
- Fallback font that maintains consistency with macOS
- Ensures UI feels native to the Mac environment

## Tone & Voice

### Personality
- **Friendly:** Approachable and welcoming
- **Clear:** No technical jargon unless absolutely necessary
- **Confident:** Assertive about the product's value
- **Helpful:** Always focused on user benefit

### Writing Guidelines
- Use simple, direct language
- Avoid technical terms when possible; when necessary, explain them
- Focus on benefits before features
- Use second person ("you") to speak directly to users
- Keep sentences and paragraphs short

## OpenClaw Relationship Guidelines

### Acceptable References
- "Works with OpenClaw technology"
- "Compatible with the OpenClaw ecosystem"
- "Simplifies installation of OpenClaw AI"
- "Makes setting up your OpenClaw assistant easier than ever"

### Avoid
- Using unofficial OpenClaw logos
- Claiming to be an official OpenClaw product
- Implying endorsement by OpenClaw creators

### Differentiators
- Focus on installation experience, not the underlying technology
- Emphasize our value-add (simplicity, support)
- Create visual identity that complements and extends OpenClaw

## Marketing Materials

### Website
- Clean, minimalist design with OpenClaw color scheme
- Focus on benefits above the fold
- Clear CTA: "Get Started for $49.99"
- Show the installation process in action

### Email Marketing
- Value-focused subject lines
- Short, scannable content
- One clear CTA per email
- Mobile-optimized templates

### Lead Magnets
- "OpenClaw AI Setup Guide" (PDF)
- "5 Ways OpenClaw AI Can Streamline Your Workflow" (Mini-course)
- "OpenClaw Setup Checklist" (Interactive PDF)
