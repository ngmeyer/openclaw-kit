# Twitter Marketing Strategy - OpenClawKit

## Goal
- **Increase follower count** from 0 to 100+ in the first week
- **Generate first sales** ($49.99 one-time purchase)
- **Build engaged community** around OpenClaw/AI assistants

## Target Audience
1. **macOS users** frustrated with terminal/CLI
2. **AI enthusiasts** using ChatGPT/Claude but want local control
3. **Developers** interested in AI agents and automation
4. **Privacy-conscious** users who want data to stay local
5. **OpenClaw community** (existing users looking for easier setup)

## Keywords to Monitor
- "OpenClaw"
- "AI assistant macOS"
- "local AI"
- "ChatGPT alternative"
- "Claude desktop"
- "macOS automation"
- "terminal frustration"
- "API key setup"
- "privacy AI"

## Daily Activities

### Morning (6-9 AM PST)
- [ ] Search 3-5 relevant keywords
- [ ] Like 10-15 tweets
- [ ] Comment on 5-7 tweets (helpful, not salesy)
- [ ] Retweet 2-3 relevant posts

### Afternoon (12-3 PM PST)
- [ ] Check mentions and reply
- [ ] Search for "just released" / "new product" in AI space
- [ ] Engage with OpenRouter, Anthropic, OpenAI community posts
- [ ] Share a tip or insight (no direct promotion)

### Evening (6-9 PM PST)
- [ ] Respond to any new mentions/DMs
- [ ] Post 1 valuable tweet (tip, use case, customer story if available)
- [ ] Engage with trending AI topics

### Weekly
- [ ] Monday: Post about ease of setup
- [ ] Wednesday: Share a customer testimonial (once we have one)
- [ ] Friday: Weekend tip/trick
- [ ] Sunday: Week in review or community highlight

## Content Themes
1. **Problem/Solution** - "Tired of X? Try Y"
2. **Behind the scenes** - Development updates
3. **User wins** - Testimonials, case studies
4. **Tips & tricks** - How to get the most from OpenClaw
5. **Industry news** - React to AI announcements with OpenClaw angle

## Engagement Rules
- ✅ Be helpful first, promotional second
- ✅ Add value in every comment
- ✅ Acknowledge and thank people who engage
- ✅ Stay professional and friendly
- ❌ Never spam
- ❌ Don't argue or get defensive
- ❌ Avoid politics unless directly relevant

## Metrics to Track
- Follower count (daily)
- Engagement rate (likes, retweets, comments)
- Profile visits
- Link clicks to openclawkit.ai
- Sales conversions
- Mentions of @OpenClawKit

## First Week Target
- **Followers:** 50-100
- **Engagement:** 20-30 interactions/day
- **Sales:** 1-3 purchases
- **Profile visits:** 200+
